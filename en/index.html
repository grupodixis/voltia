IoT for agriculture and industry

Sensing is not about adding devices. It is about turning distributed assets into operational intelligence.

We combine sensors, gateways, panels, communications and platforms so an estate, a warehouse, a plant room or a production line can be measured and managed without relying on constant site visits or isolated data.

Agriculture

Land, water, energy and primary production

Telemetry for irrigation, wells, tanks, pumping systems, cold rooms, greenhouses, silos, weather stations, electricity use and isolated machinery.

  • Soil moisture, temperature, rainfall, wind and solar radiation
  • Tank levels, flow, pressure, leaks and pump status
  • Irrigation control by zone, schedule, weather and water availability
  • Alerts for frost, low pressure, abnormal consumption or electrical faults
Industry

Facilities, processes, machinery and maintenance

Industrial IoT reveals what is happening in panels, lines, motors, compressors, cold rooms, ovens, warehouses, access systems and plant rooms.

  • Vibration, temperature, energy, machine hours and duty cycles
  • Panel alarms, trips, contactor status and variable-speed drives
  • Monitoring for industrial refrigeration, humidity, CO2, air quality and pressure
  • Predictive maintenance, basic OEE, historical data and automated reports
Sensing Sensors and actuators

Temperature, humidity, energy, flow, pressure, level, vibration, presence, opening, CO2, air quality, relays, valves and contactors.

Communications Gateways and networks

Platform Data, alarms and dashboards

Web dashboards, historical data, rules, maps, user roles, email and mobile alerts, exports and ERP or maintenance integration.

Decisions Automation and AI
